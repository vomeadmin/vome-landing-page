# Vome — Plans Feature Comparison Matrix

A complete reference of every feature surfaced in the **Feature comparison by plan** section of the [/plans](src/pages/plans.astro) page, organized exactly as it appears in [PlansBody.astro](src/components/content/PlansBody.astro). For each feature you'll find:

- The **section** it belongs to (matrix divider row)
- The **feature name** (visible label in the table)
- The **description** shown when a user hovers/focuses the info-circle helper
- **Per-plan availability** across Recruit, Pro, Enterprise, and Ultimate

**Legend**

- ✅ — Included
- ❌ — Not included
- A text value (e.g. `Unlimited`, `1 form`, `Basic`, `Standard`, `Priority`) means the feature is included with that limit/tier on that plan

> **Footnote (from the live page):** Onboarding sessions & account review sessions are capped at a maximum of 2 hours per week on the Ultimate plan ("Unlimited \*").

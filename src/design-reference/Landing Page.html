So many ways to track volunteer hours. Pick what fits your program.

Multiple ways to check in

QR scan, tap-to-check-in, on-site kiosk, or fully automatic check-in and check-out. Pick one, or mix different methods across your different volunteer programs.

Proximity validation for attendance integrity

Require volunteers to be physically within a defined radius to check in or out. Customizable by program. Prevents remote check-ins and protects program data.

Walk-ins, group reservations, and off-site hours

On-site kiosks handle check-in and check-out for everyone on a shift, with an optional "Join a shift" mode for walk-ins who want to sign up on the spot. Group reservations roll up at the group level for reporting. Volunteers can also submit hour claims for work they did off-platform.

Audit-ready hour reports

Shifts and hours by volunteer, a full reservations and hours log, and custom database exports. Every hour traced to a person, a shift, and a check-in method.

Frequently asked questions

Everything coordinators ask before switching to Vome. Still have questions? Talk to our team →

How long does it take to get set up?
Most organizations are fully onboarded within two weeks. Our migration team handles importing your existing volunteer database, shifts, and history, and trains your coordinators before you go live. Larger enterprises with multi-site deployments typically complete rollout in 30–45 days.
Do you offer a free trial?
We offer a free, personalized demo where we walk through Vome with your specific program in mind. If you'd like to try it with real data, we can set up a sandboxed trial account after the demo so you can test workflows with your team before committing.
Can I import data from my current system?
Yes. We migrate volunteer records, hours history, program structures, and custom fields from tools like VolunteerHub, Better Impact, SignUpGenius, Galaxy Digital, and spreadsheets. Our onboarding team handles the whole migration so nothing is lost.
Is my volunteer data secure?
Vome is HIPAA, GDPR, PIPEDA, CCPA, and PCI DSS compliant, with SOC 2 Type II underway. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit, hosted on SOC 2–certified infrastructure, and backed by role-based access controls, audit logs, and SSO for enterprise plans.
How does pricing work?
Pricing is based on your number of active volunteers and the modules you need. We offer tiered plans for single-site nonprofits, multi-site organizations, and enterprise health systems. Reach out for a quote tailored to your program.
Do volunteers need to download an app?
The Vome app is available on iOS and Android and is the best experience for volunteers, but every feature also works on the web. Volunteers can browse opportunities, sign up, and check in directly from a browser if they prefer.
Can Vome support multiple locations and programs?
Yes. Vome is built for multi-site and multi-program organizations. Each location can have its own coordinators, programs, and shifts while rolling up into a unified dashboard for regional or organization-wide reporting.
